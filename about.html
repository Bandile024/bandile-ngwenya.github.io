About Me

I am Bandile Ngwenya, a passionate website developer with a roots in Johannesburg, South Africa. My career began with a curiosity for coding, evolving into a professional pursuit of excellence in web development. I hold a solid command of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, complemented by hands-on experience with React, Bootstrap, and other cutting-edge tools. My mission is to design and develop websites that are not only visually stunning but also highly functional and accessible.

With a self-taught foundation and ongoing education, I have completed several short courses in web technologies, enhancing my ability to create responsive designs and optimize user experiences. I am committed to staying abreast of industry trends, such as progressive web apps and AI-driven design, to deliver innovative solutions. My portfolio includes custom websites for small businesses and personal projects, reflecting my dedication to craftsmanship in every line of code.
